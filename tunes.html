Tracks You Can Sip To

There's nothing like sitting and contemplating with a cup of coffee or tea (or cocktail) in your hand. Here are some grooves for you to sip and contemplate to. Enjoy!

Rise and Shine

To start the day off with a loud bang coffee drinkers can try Days Like This by Shaun Escoffrey. Its a house music jam that is sure to wake you right up.

Tea Lovers can unwind a little more slowly while listening and contemplating with Spark by Royksopp.

Jazz It Up

Sunflowers by the outstanding Wynton Marsalis Septet is the perfect sippking interlude. Try sipping your favorite latte or herbal tea to this tune.

Latin Jazz by Putumayo Presents are Afro-Cuban rhythms and jazz stylings that have come together on this lively collection of songs by the masters of Latin Jazz. Sip Away!

No sippling jazz collection is complete without the masterful compositions on the 1964 classic Love Supreme by John Coltrane

Unclassic Classical

Take a wild ride while you sip listening to Gypsy Rhapsody by Bond. The sexy string quartet that are definitely not your mama's classical group.

Chill out to Magic by Black Violin. A radical fusion of hip-hop and pop with classical music that create a kaleidoscope of sounds that are uniquely their own.

Let Aurora unfold by revelutionary violinist Vanessa Mae

Lounge

Lay back and enjoy French and Brazillian melodies on Saudade by Thievery Corporation.

Sittin Here by St. Germain. Enough Said.

The Best of Buddha Bar simply stated, the is compilation is awesome.

International Flavor

For the days and nights that you you want that rare meld of quietude and intensity look no further than Chan Chan by Buena Bista Social Club. Cuban music at it's best.

Take a trip to Paris with Putumayo Paris Cafe.
